About us Adding Ability provides multidisciplinary rehabilitation services for children having neurodevelopmental, neuropsychiatric, psychological and language disorders.
International Classification of Functioning, Disability, and Health (ICF)
Our  therapist uses the International Classification of Functioning, Disability, and Health (ICF) model in a problem solving approach to assess activity and participation, thereby to identify and prioritize relevant integrities and impairments as a basis for establishing achievable outcomes with clients and caregivers. Several Assessment Tools are used as an indicator of capacity and  challenges under this model.
Our therapist have  in-depth knowledge of the human movement system, including the understanding of typical and atypical development, and have expertise in analyzing development milestone, sensory processing, postural control, movement, activity, and participation
